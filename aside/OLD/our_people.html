Dr. Lukas Fecker is our Founder and CEO. He is the Chairman of Turnaround Management Association (TMA) Europe. Lukas specializes in Banking Turnaround. He focuses on Investments in private asset management companies, work-out banking and distressed investing. Lukas has over 20 years of experience and was a Partner at EY & KPMG and a Director at Alvarez and Marsal. He led EY’s Banking Turnaround Solution EMEIA. Besides advisory, Lukas has been in trusted senior executive positions with 6 major businesses in turnaround as a CRO, CEO or board member.
Some of Lukas’ significant achievements include the scheme for a CHF25bn turnover stock listed business, the restructuring of a leading European Bank, and the restructuring of 2 funds.
