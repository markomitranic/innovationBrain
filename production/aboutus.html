Innovation Brain has over 20 best industry experts in restructuring and turnaround.

All our senior executives have held board and management positions within various companies and have a track record to have built multi-billion dollar businesses. We recognise the vitalness of working collaboratively with our clients – to seek out the best value for and from their business. Our client relationships are built on mutual trust and partnership – handled with discretion and with always having their best interests in mind.

Our experts span a wide range of industries and specialise in three core sectors:

Most recently, our team have been appointed by the European Commission appointed to monitor the restructuring of the largest bank in a EU member country.

We step in when we see an opportunity to acquire a complex group, bring innovation and lead them to the next growth cycle as a whole or in independent parts.

At Innovation Brain we specialise in the business of restructuring. We seek to create value at the crossroads of a business’ lifecycle, where the future rests on bold decisions and innovative solutions.

Along with our wealth of expertise, we bring you the ability to derive value from your key, non performing and non core assets, through turnaround. Organisations ask us to step in when:

  • in need for strong executives, investment capacity and advisory
  • seeking an equity partner in a restructuring and turnaround
  • in need for innovation and new ideas when all other solutions have failed
